Our Story

"When you know, you just know."

" Elisabeth insists that, in October of 2014, she was at her wits end with online dating; the medium through which so many single 20-something's are compelled to meet in the new millennium."
"Rafael had arrived in Philly from Brasil that fall, and reached out to Elisabeth upon discovering her amidst the online dating chaos."
"One evening in October, a jaded Elisabeth and a bright eyed Rafael met for a drink at the Franklin Mortgage Exchange. Everything took off from there!"
"An early November night at the Art Museum lead to the two agreeing they wanted to be "official." Later, Rafael joined the Frankenbach family in the Poconos for Christmas, where he passed the wood chopping test/right of passage."
