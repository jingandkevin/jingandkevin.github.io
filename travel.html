501 Union

This historic venue, first erected in 1916, is a space of subtle distinction and quiet refinement. Uniquely situated at the hub of five distinctly stylish Brooklyn neighborhoods, the space features a lounge which offers plenty of room to mingle around a 15 ft Carrara marble top bar, detailed woodwork, brass fixtures, and a lustrous white tin ceiling lit with suspended pendant fixtures; a garden which allows for outdoor seating and socializing and walls of glass that open onto Union St.; and the gallery, a 3,500 sq. ft space featuring lofted ceilings and a custom-built 50 ft skylight wrapped in exposed brick/hardwoods and illuminated by Jason Miller Modo chandeliers and sconces.
